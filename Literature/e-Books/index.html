e-Books

Use an e-Book manager like Calibre to manage and convert your e-Books, it provides a good interface.

General E-Books

  • Z-Library - The Library is only accessible through Tor and I2P, Requires Sign-up.
  • Library Genesis (.fun) & .rs | .is | .st - The .fun domain is currently being run by the original owner of libgen whereas the others are independant.
  • Mobilism (.me) | .org - Forum, Requires Sign-up.
  • MYWAREZ.ORG - Forum, Requires Sign-up.
  • The-eye - Many e-Books and calibre libraries.
  • eBook Hunter
  • Ebookee
  • OceanofPDF
  • PDF Drive
  • Ikindleebooks - MOBIs and ePUBs for Kindle.
  • My Anonamouse - Has a lot of content, including niche e-Books and audiobooks. Friendly community and mods. Pretty easy to get into.
  • Project Gutenberg - Legally free e-Books.  
     

Audio Books

  • Mobilism (.me) | .org - Forum, Requires Sign-up.
  • AudioBookBay
  • XAudioBooks
  • Galaxy Audiobooks
  • MYWAREZ.ORG - Audiobooks section of a Forum, Requires Sign-up.
  • LibriVox - Legally free audiobooks  
     

Scientific Journals/Technical Manuals

  • Sci-Hub (.se) | .st | .ru
            Sci-Hub X Now! - Opens the Sci-Hub page for the article you want to read.
            Zotero Scihub - Addon for Zotero and Juris-M to automatically download PDFs from Sci-Hub.
  • SciMag - Scientific Articles section of LibGen.
  • Free Full PDF
  • Freetechbooks
  • r/Scholar - If you cant find an article anywhere, you can request it in this subreddit.  
     

Other

  • Rave Book Search Engine - A search engine that indexes many Comic, e-Book and Audiobook sites.
  • CALISHOT - A search engine indexing many open Calibre servers, also has a Non English Books Section.
    If the links stop working check here for updated ones.
  • Archive.org & Open Library - Has a lot of niche historical content.
  • Trantor - Darkweb e-Book resource.
  • Flibusta - Russian e-Book site.
  • Yudhacookbook - Contains many recipes and cookbooks.  
     

Tools

  • Bypass Paywalls Clean - Bypass News Article paywalls.
    A more actively maintained fork (with no Google analytics) of Bypass Paywalls.
  • Unpaywall Browser Extension: Chromium & Firefox - When you view a research article, Unpaywall automatically looks for a copy in its index. If it finds one, click the green tab to read the article.
  • Outline - Removes clutter from News Articles.
  • Open Access Button - Search engine for research articles.
            Browser Extension Chromium & Firefox
  • Libgen Desktop - Windows application for browsing a local copy of the LibGen catalog.  
     

IRC

  • IRChighway.net/eBooks - Use this guide or their official FAQ to learn how to use the commands.
